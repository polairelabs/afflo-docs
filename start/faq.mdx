Do I need to code?
- No. Everything is point‑and‑click. Any developer tools (like Supabase) are used behind the scenes only.

Which stores are supported?
- Shopify is supported today. TikTok Shop is available on select plans.

How do I pay affiliates?
- Use the Payouts page. You can connect payment tools like Tremendous or Trolley.

Can my team collaborate?
- Yes. Invite teammates under Settings → Team and set their permissions.

How do creators join?
- Share your sign‑up link or invite creators directly from Affiliates or Programs.
